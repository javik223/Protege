Human Resources

Human Resources

At Protege Professionals, we work with organizations to improve their human capacity and create streamlined strategies and actions to source, recruit and retain the best talents that will improve their organizations.

Our HR services encompass all areas of the recruitment and developmental phases of employment.

HR Outsourcing

All organisations that want to thrive need Executive HR presence in the boardroom. The role of HR is not simply personnel management but organisational strategy management. We save the time spent on admin by offering a suite of outsourcing packages that best suit you.

HR Audit

If you already have a HR department, we can help you “take stock” of your organisation through our comprehensive HR Audit. An organisation goes through four stages of transition characterised by distinct HR issues. If you want to know where your organisation stands, call us today and take our survey.

Recruitment & Selection

We understand the challenges organisations face in finding and choosing the right candidates. Let us take that burden off you. Protégé Professionals specialises not just in recruitment and selection, but also the training of these protégés through our finishing school. We work closely with clients and candidates to ensure they are the right fit. To do this effectively, we follow a comprehensive selection process.
